Virtual Art Exhibition

Exhibition Overview

Welcome to Cindy's Creative Collective. This virtual art exhibition holds various works from Cindy, ranging from traditional mixed media to 2D and digitally manipulated artwork. The purpose of this exhibition is to create a digital space for the artist to look back on her progress and development as an artist. There is a sense of nostalgia and some diversity in her works that are placed and curated in this seemingly traditional space.
